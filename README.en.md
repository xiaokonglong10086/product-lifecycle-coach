# Product Lifecycle Coach

A Chinese-first ChatGPT Skill for solo product managers and early-stage product builders. It supports discovery, requirement clarification, readable product-function PRDs, AI feature specifications, Codex delivery tasks, QA, pilots, and production handoff.

The project focuses on preserving approved product semantics, keeping product documents readable, separating product behavior from engineering implementation, and scaling rigor to risk.
